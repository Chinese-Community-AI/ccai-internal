## Before you sign

- No contracts without Operations / Legal review
- NDAs: use standard template when possible
- Sponsorships: disclose conflicts of interest

## Privacy basics

- Minimize collection of personal data
- Document data flows for new features (Product + Eng)
- Honor deletion requests per policy

## Open source

- Confirm license compatibility before importing dependencies
- Contributor license agreement for major external contributions (when applicable)
