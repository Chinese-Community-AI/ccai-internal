Build shared engineering practices for designing, testing, operating, and documenting CCAI systems.

## Expectations (first 30 days)

- Ship at least one small PR that includes or improves tests
- Read [Fundamentals of Testing](/wiki/engineering-bootcamp/testing-guide/fundamentals-of-testing/overview)
- Shadow on-call if your team runs a rotation
