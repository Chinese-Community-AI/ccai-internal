## Role summary

Campus leads represent CCAI at their school: host study groups, promote events, and gather feedback.

## Onboarding

1. Complete [General Onboarding](/wiki/general-onboarding/overview) (abbreviated student track)
2. Join `#campus-leads`
3. Attend monthly campus sync (30 min)
4. Co-host one small event in first 60 days

## Support from CCAI

| Support | How to request |
| ------- | -------------- |
| Swag / stickers | Campus Programs, 3 weeks lead |
| Speakers | `#community` with date + topic |
| Funding | Partnerships + Operations approval |

## Expectations

- Follow [Code of Conduct](/wiki/volunteer-contributor/code-of-conduct)
- No exclusive claims (“official club of vendor X”) without approval
- Report attendance metrics after each event
