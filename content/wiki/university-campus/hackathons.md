## Timeline (6–8 weeks)

| Week | Milestone |
| ---- | --------- |
| 1–2 | Venue, date, theme, budget draft |
| 3–4 | Registration live, mentors recruited |
| 5 | Workshops / team formation |
| 6 | Hack weekend + judging |
| 7 | Winners announced, survey sent |
| 8 | Retro wiki PR + thank-you posts |

## Judging criteria (example)

| Criterion | Weight |
| --------- | ------ |
| Impact for Chinese-speaking users | 30% |
| Technical execution | 30% |
| Demo clarity | 20% |
| Responsible AI (safety, privacy) | 20% |
