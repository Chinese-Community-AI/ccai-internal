## Day 1

- [ ] Complete HR paperwork and payroll setup
- [ ] Join Slack / Discord workspace channels (`#announcements`, `#help`)
- [ ] Read [Tools & Access](/wiki/general-onboarding/tools-and-access)
- [ ] Meet your onboarding buddy (30 min intro)

## Day 2–3

- [ ] Attend org overview (mission, teams, current priorities)
- [ ] Set up dev environment **or** role tools (see your bootcamp)
- [ ] Skim the wiki home and bookmark your bootcamp hub

## Day 4–5

- [ ] Shadow a standup or planning meeting
- [ ] Pick one small doc improvement PR (typos, clarifications welcome)
- [ ] Schedule 1:1 with your manager for expectations and 30/60/90 goals

## Questions?

Ask in `#help` or your onboarding buddy. For access issues, tag **Operations & Admin**.
