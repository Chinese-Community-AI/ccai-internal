## Code of conduct (summary)

Be respectful, no harassment, no discriminatory language. Debate ideas, not people.

## Escalation ladder

1. **Reminder** — public or DM, neutral tone
2. **Temporary mute** — cooling-off period
3. **Removal** — serious or repeated violations
4. **Leadership review** — legal/safety concerns

## Documentation

Log incidents privately (Operations template). Do not debate moderation decisions in public threads.
