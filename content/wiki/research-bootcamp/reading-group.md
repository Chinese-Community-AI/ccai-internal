## Paper selection

- Rotate facilitators; propose papers 1 week ahead in `#research`
- Mix foundational, applied, and community-relevant work (incl. Chinese-language sources when valuable)
- Prefer open-access PDFs or official arXiv links

## Presentation template (30–45 min)

1. **Problem & motivation** (5 min)
2. **Method** (10 min) — diagrams welcome
3. **Results & limitations** (10 min)
4. **Discussion** (15 min) — “what would we try at CCAI?”

## Notes

- Record slides/notes in the wiki via PR when possible
- Tag related product or safety docs if the paper affects shipping decisions
