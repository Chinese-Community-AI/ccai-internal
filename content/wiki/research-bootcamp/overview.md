For members exploring papers, reproducibility, benchmarks, and research collaborations at CCAI.

## Modules

- [Reading Group](/wiki/research-bootcamp/reading-group) — how we read and present papers
- [Benchmarks & Evals](/wiki/research-bootcamp/benchmarks) — running and reporting evaluations

## Expectations

- Cite sources and note limitations clearly in talks and posts
- Share reproducibility notes (data, code, compute) when presenting results
- Coordinate with **AI Safety & Policy** for user-facing or high-risk demos

## Cadence

| Activity | Frequency |
| -------- | --------- |
| Paper reading | Biweekly |
| Lightning talks | Monthly |
| Benchmark sync | As needed for product launches |
