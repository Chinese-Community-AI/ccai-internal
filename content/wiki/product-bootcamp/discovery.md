## Problem framing template

1. **Who** is affected? (persona, segment)
2. **What** pain are they feeling? (observable behavior)
3. **Why now**? (urgency, strategic fit)
4. **How might we** … ? (hypothesis, not solution yet)

## Research methods

| Method | When to use |
| ------ | ----------- |
| User interviews | New problem spaces, 5–8 participants |
| Survey | Quant validation, large community samples |
| Data review | Funnel drops, feature adoption |
| Dogfooding | Internal tools, early prototypes |

## Handoff to engineering

Bring a one-pager with problem, success metrics, and out-of-scope before asking for estimates.
