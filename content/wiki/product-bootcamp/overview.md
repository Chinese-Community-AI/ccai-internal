How we define problems, prioritize work, and partner with engineering and design at CCAI.

## Modules

- [Discovery & Research](/wiki/product-bootcamp/discovery) — interviews, problem framing
- [Writing Specs](/wiki/product-bootcamp/specs) — PRDs, acceptance criteria, rollout

## Rituals

| Ritual | Cadence | Output |
| ------ | ------- | ------ |
| Product review | Weekly | Prioritized backlog |
| Roadmap check-in | Monthly | Themes & metrics |
| Launch retro | Per release | Learnings doc |

## Key metrics we track

- Activation and retention for community products
- Contributor growth (events, content, open source)
- Qualitative feedback from user interviews
