## Positioning

**Chinese Community AI** connects builders, researchers, and enthusiasts who advance AI for Chinese-speaking communities worldwide.

## Voice principles

| Principle | Example |
| --------- | ------- |
| Welcoming | “Join us” not “Apply now or miss out” |
| Precise | Name the audience and outcome |
| Bilingual-aware | Prefer terms your audience actually uses (简体 / 繁體 as appropriate) |
| Humble on hype | Avoid unfounded “SOTA” claims |

## Visual basics

- Use approved logo assets only (ask Marketing for the kit)
- Maintain contrast for accessibility on social graphics
- Event materials: include date, timezone, and registration link
