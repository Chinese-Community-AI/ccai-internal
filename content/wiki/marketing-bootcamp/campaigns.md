## Campaign brief template

1. **Objective** (awareness, signups, contributions)
2. **Audience** (geo, language, persona)
3. **Message** (one sentence)
4. **Channels & timeline**
5. **Budget** (if any)
6. **Success metrics** (UTM, registrations, etc.)

## Launch checklist

- [ ] Copy approved (EN + 中文 if applicable)
- [ ] Creative assets in Figma / drive
- [ ] Landing page live and tested on mobile
- [ ] Analytics / UTM parameters set
- [ ] Community team briefed for Q&A

## Post-campaign

Run a short retro: what worked, what to repeat, list of follow-up content ideas for the wiki.
