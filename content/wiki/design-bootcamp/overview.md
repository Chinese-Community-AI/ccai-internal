## Modules

- [Design System](/wiki/design-bootcamp/design-system) — tokens, components, accessibility
- [Critique & Handoff](/wiki/design-bootcamp/critique) — reviews, specs for engineering

## Design principles

1. **Inclusive** — readable type, color contrast, bilingual layouts where needed
2. **Consistent** — reuse components before inventing new patterns
3. **Prototype early** — Figma flows before high-fidelity polish
