## Critique format

- **Context** — user, scenario, constraints
- **What works** — specific, tied to goals
- **Questions** — open, not prescriptive commands
- **Next step** — owner + date

## Engineering handoff checklist

- [ ] Figma dev mode links on all frames
- [ ] Redlines or tokens for spacing/type
- [ ] Empty, loading, and error states
- [ ] Responsive breakpoints documented
- [ ] Assets exported (SVG/PNG) or linked
