## When to run an experiment

- Clear hypothesis and primary metric
- Enough traffic for statistical power (ask Data to size)
- Rollback plan documented

## Ethics & privacy

- No experiments on sensitive attributes without review
- Inform users when required by policy
- Prefer opt-in betas for high-risk changes

## Readout template

1. Hypothesis
2. Results (metric + confidence)
3. Decision (ship / iterate / kill)
4. Follow-ups
