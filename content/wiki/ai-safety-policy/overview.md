Responsible AI practices for CCAI products, research demos, and public programs.

## Modules

- [Product Guidelines](/wiki/ai-safety-policy/guidelines) — what we ship and how we message it
- [Safety Reviews](/wiki/ai-safety-policy/reviews) — when and how to get a review

## Scope

Applies to:

- User-facing apps and bots
- Public model demos and leaderboards
- Training materials that instruct others to deploy models

## Escalation

High-risk proposals (synthetic media, minors, regulated advice) require Safety Council sign-off before launch.
