## Partnership types

| Type | Examples |
| ---- | -------- |
| Student org | AI/CS clubs, hackathon hosts |
| Faculty | Guest lectures, research collabs |
| Career | Info sessions, internship pipelines |

## Outreach template

- Introduce CCAI mission and past events in the region
- Offer co-branded workshop or hackathon (see [Events Playbook](/wiki/community-bootcamp/events))
- Provide speaker list and code-of-conduct summary upfront

## Do / Don’t

| Do | Don’t |
| -- | ----- |
| Localize time zones and language | Assume all students read English fluently |
| Credit partner logos per brand guidelines | Promise job placements without HR involvement |
| Track attendance for follow-up | Share student emails without consent |
