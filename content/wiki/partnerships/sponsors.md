## Sponsor tiers (example)

| Tier | Benefits | Typical ask |
| ---- | -------- | ----------- |
| Community | Logo on event page | In-kind (venue, credits) |
| Gold | Talk slot + booth | Cash + brand review |
| Strategic | Multi-event series | Co-created program, advisory |

## Deliverables checklist

- [ ] Signed agreement (Operations / Legal)
- [ ] Logo assets and speaking points approved
- [ ] Disclosure on website and opening remarks
- [ ] Post-event metrics report (Marketing + Data)

## Conflicts

Decline or escalate sponsorships that conflict with CCAI values or **AI Safety & Policy** guidelines.
