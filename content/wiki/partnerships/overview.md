How CCAI works with universities, sponsors, and ecosystem allies.

## Modules

- [Universities](/wiki/partnerships/universities) — campus clubs, faculty, joint events
- [Sponsors & Allies](/wiki/partnerships/sponsors) — funding, in-kind, co-marketing

## Principles

1. **Mutual value** — clear benefits for students, CCAI, and partners
2. **Transparency** — disclose sponsorship in talks and materials
3. **No lock-in** — prefer agreements that respect open community norms

## Intake process

1. Initial call (Partnerships + relevant bootcamp lead)
2. Scope doc: goals, timeline, brand usage, data sharing
3. Operations + Legal review for contracts
4. Launch plan with Marketing & Community

See also [University & Campus](/wiki/university-campus/overview) for student-led programs.
